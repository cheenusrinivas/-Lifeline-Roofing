Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about our services, pricing, or process? You're not alone. Here are answers to some of the most common questions we get from customers before starting a project.

1. How much does a roofing or masonry job usually cost?

Every project is unique, but we provide free assessments and clear, upfront quotes. Pricing depends on materials, scope, and size — with no hidden fees.

2. How quickly can you start once I approve the quote?

We typically begin within 3–7 business days depending on availability and weather. For urgent repairs, we try to accommodate same-week service.

3. Do you handle permits or paperwork for the job?

Yes, if your project requires permits (like chimney rebuilds or structural changes), we’ll guide you through the process and help ensure everything is approved and compliant.

4. Can I book a free inspection or quote?

Absolutely! We offer free site visits and assessments. Just give us a call or fill out the contact form — we’ll schedule a time that works best for you.

5. Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes. We stand behind our work with a workmanship guarantee and only use high-quality, trusted materials for long-lasting results.
